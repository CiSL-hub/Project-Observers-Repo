
The realm of the [Origin Gods](https://www.notion.so/7671757f00604eb0b40727556bcbf819?pvs=21), outside of the reality bubble made by Ci. The origin of the [Primordial Gods](https://www.notion.so/7671757f00604eb0b40727556bcbf819?pvs=21), the ancient world, the fuel for a collosal change.

Not much is known besides it being the home of the [Primordial Gods](https://www.notion.so/7671757f00604eb0b40727556bcbf819?pvs=21). None of its whereabouts, condition, nor existence itself could be proven aside from tall tales and stories from its former inhabitants.

It is believed that the [eclipsed core](https://www.notion.so/The-Hollow-Sun-278e1200618080098b04d79b9d2295c6?pvs=21) originates here, along with the [Primordial Gods](https://www.notion.so/7671757f00604eb0b40727556bcbf819?pvs=21) and whatever exists before them.
