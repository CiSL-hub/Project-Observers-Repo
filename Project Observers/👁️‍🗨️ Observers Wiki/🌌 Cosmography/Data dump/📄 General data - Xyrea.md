
### **Base Properties**

- **Composition:** Mottled yellow-orange (EXA) + pale blue-gray (DEA)
    
- **Mass:** 1 × Moon mass (~7.35 × 10²² kg)
    
- **Diameter:** ~3,480 km
    
- **Orbit:** ~310,000 km from Zordurix, inclined 1–2°
    
- **Orbital direction:** Retrograde relative DEA’s previous prograde orbit (Still essentially retrograde like EXA, because EXA’s mass dominates and “carries” the orbit)
    
- **Tides:** Slightly stronger than original DEA/EXA combination
    
- **Formation:** Result of catastrophic collision between EXA and DEA
