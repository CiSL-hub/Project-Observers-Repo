
### **Base Properties**

- **Origin:** Debris from EXA–DEA collision
    
- **Inclination:** 1–2° relative to Zordurix’s equator
    
- **Width:** Tens of thousands of km
    
- **Thickness:** ~100–200 km
    
- **Appearance:** Sparkling blue-gold arcs visible at night, reminiscent of both moons’ original colors
    
- **Stability:** Expected to last hundreds to thousands of years before partial accretion or dispersion
