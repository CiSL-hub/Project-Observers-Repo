
### Base properties

- **Diameter:**
    
    ~105,000 light-years
    
- **Thickness (disk):**
    
    ~1,200 light-years
    
- **Star count:**
    
    ~180–220 billion
    
- **Core:**
    
    ~Supermassive black hole
    
- **Collective mass:**
    
    ~4.3 million solar masses
    

### **The Inner Bar**

- Dense stellar traffic
- High metallicity
- Old civilizations tend to emerge here… and collapse early

### **Spiral Arms**

- 4 total, none mirrors each other (tampered creation)
- Feather arms are fragmented and forming stars inconsistently

### **Galactic Halo**

- Sparse globular clusters
- **Too few satellite dwarf galaxies (sparks scientific controversy)**
- Stellar streams that don’t trace clean gravitational history (like something supposed to be there… isn’t)

### COMPOSITION (AVERAGE)

- Hydrogen & helium dominated
- Metallicity slightly _too uniform_
- Older stars have elements they “shouldn’t” (e.g. heavier elements on earlier time frames)
