
### Base properties

- **Radius:**
    
    ~1.21 Earth (7645 KM)
    
- **Mass:**
    
    ~1.68 Earth Masses (1.003×10^25 kg)
    
- **Gravity:**
    
    ~1.17g (11.45 m/s^2)
    
- **Orbit:**
    
    ~0.59 AU from its star
    
- Parent Star ([[☀️ Aurethys]])
    
    ~K2V (~4900 K)
    
- **Axial Tilt**
    
    ~23° (Similar to earth)
    
- **Moons:**
    
    ~Strong tides
    
- **Axial wobble:**
    
    ~Slow, historical
    
- **Axial procession:**
    
    ~18.000 years (as opposed to earth’s 26.000)
    
- **Day length:**
    
    ~26.4 hours (as opposed to earth’s 23.93 h) (1 day in Zordurix is 1.1 on Earth)
    
- **Year length:**
    
    ~161 local days
    

### Tides

- Strong daily tides
- Irregular long surges
- Star–moon resonance effects
- Stellar tidal pull ≈ **~3× Earth’s solar tide**

### Seasons

- Earth-like tilt (4 seasons)
- Cold times are less - Hot times more intense (Spring feels urgent instead of relaxing for example)
- Faster seasonal rhythm
- Faster calendar decay (compared to the earth)
