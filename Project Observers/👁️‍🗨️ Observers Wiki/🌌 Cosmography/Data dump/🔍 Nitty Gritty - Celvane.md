## Color & shimmer zones

- **“Color shimmer zones”** = how debris composition creates **subtle color gradients** along the ring depending on angle and sunlight:

|Zone|Source|Visual effect|
|---|---|---|
|Inner edge|DEA debris (blue-gray)|faint bluish shimmer closest to planet|
|Outer edge|EXA debris (yellow-orange)|warm golden glint on outer edge|
|Dust clusters|ice/dust|fine sparkling dots all along the ring|
- Depending on viewing angle and Sun position, these zones **mix** → creates the dual-color sparkling effect


### Day vs Twilight vs Night

| Time of day        | Sky brightness                     | Celvane visibility                                              |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Midday             | Very bright (scattering dominates) | faint shimmer only at perfect angles                            |
| Twilight (sun low) | Sky dimming                        | rings **glow dramatically**, almost magical, dual-color shimmer |
| Night              | Sky dark                           | faint glowing arcs, subtle sparkle, colors muted                |
