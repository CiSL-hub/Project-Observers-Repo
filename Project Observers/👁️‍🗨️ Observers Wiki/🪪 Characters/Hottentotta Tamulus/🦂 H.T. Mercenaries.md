### Name origin

- Hottentotta Tamulus 
- _**Hottentotta**_ is a [genus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Genus "Genus") of [scorpions](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scorpion "Scorpion") of the family [Buthidae](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buthidae "Buthidae"). It is distributed widely across [Africa](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Africa "Africa"), except for most of the [Sahara](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sahara "Sahara") desert. Species in the genus also occur in the [Middle East](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Middle_East "Middle East"), the [Arabian Peninsula](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arabian_Peninsula "Arabian Peninsula"), southeastern [Turkey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Turkey "Turkey"), [Iraq](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iraq "Iraq"), [Iran](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Iran "Iran"), [Afghanistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Afghanistan "Afghanistan"), [Pakistan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pakistan "Pakistan"), [India](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/India "India"), [Nepal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nepal "Nepal"), [Cape Verde Islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cape_Verde_Islands "Cape Verde Islands"), and [Sri Lanka](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sri_Lanka "Sri Lanka") (introduced).
