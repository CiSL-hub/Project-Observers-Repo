
Yukii is a lingering spirit of the only daughter of a late bourgeois family that got wiped during one winter. Roaming the alps with pain and obsession over unfairness keeping her bound and blind, manifesting as a restless banshee of sorts, haunting anyone who passes by until given closure one day, and decided to atone by living a new life with reason and discipline guiding her path.
