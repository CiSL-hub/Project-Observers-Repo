
A former Adventurer who lost his friend on the journey due to recklessness. The scar made him live a solitude bounty hunter life. Although, his carefree soul lets him mesh with just anyone, he refuses to hold dear anyone to prevent the same thing from happening again.

### Trivia

- “Your story ends here” is what he always said before finishing his foes. It was seen as cringe and cheesy, but Haru says it nonetheless.
