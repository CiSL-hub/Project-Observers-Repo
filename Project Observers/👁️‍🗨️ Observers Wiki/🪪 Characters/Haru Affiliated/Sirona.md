
A blue haired girl adopted into the 'Si' household after found wandering alone near a riverbed on the eastern mountains.

---

Much about Sirona is hazy or unknown, even to herself.
Sirona couldn't remember what her past or purpose were, constantly confused and lost while drifting towards a feeling that pulls her to seemingly nothing and nowhere, everytime.

She knows not of the attractor, but recognize that it's the only thing that keeps her wandering for who knows how long.
